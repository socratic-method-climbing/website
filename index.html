About Me

My name is Roman Rogowski. I've been climbing for over ten years, and I have a passion for analyzing the movements, behaviors, and mindsets of climbers.

I believe that climbers can improve not only through strength training, but also via the practice of deep self-inquiry—asking more to achieve more.

As your coach, I'll teach you to analyze your performance, ask better questions, and find your own path to progress. Let's find out what you're really capable of.
